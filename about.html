Get To Know Us

What We're All About

Welcome to Bean Soup and the Rat Bastards, a once-a-month in-person book club. Each month, a different member picks the title and leads the discussion. We explore a wide range of genres and voices and emphasize works by underrepresented authors and communities. Whethere we're diving deep into queer fantasy or dissecting gripping memories, we bring curiosity, candor, and compassion to our conversations (and plenty of snacks and bevvies).

Our Mission and History

We started BSRB on a whim, but it quickly grew into an important community circle for us. What began with just three members has grown over time. We're still a small group, but a dedicated and close-knit one. Our mission is to create a welcoming, judgement-free space for thoughtful conversation, mutual support, and a shared love of stories that inspire, amaze, or just make us laugh.
